Drag Racing

  • High Concept: A fast, timed racing game where you and your friend speed down the roadway, 1st place is the only goal.
  • Theme: Chickens because they're everywhere in the desert.
  • Mandated Variety: I used keyboard input, used the racing game genre. There is no randomness, only skills.
  • Prototype Goal: The main game mechanic I wanted to test was "The Hot Potato" as I wanted the game to force players to not just continuously hold the accelerate button. But that was not implemented yet.
  • Player Experience Goals: A fast, fun experience.
  • Gameplay: The player can drive the car forward by pressing W or the Up Arrow Key. I wanted to have a speedometer that showed whether the car was going to overheat when the player held their acceleration key for too long. I also wanted a minimap to show the distance and have the maps much longer.
  • Strategies: Timing and reflexes as the first player to accelerate first wins.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Chickens enjoy watching drag racing, so you and your friends race to entertain them in order to save the world.
  • Target Audience: To all the racers out there that just want to go fast.
  • Play Time: Less than a minute.
